The Crisis of Noncrisis: The Sorrow of Being an Arsenal Fan

by Brian Phillips

Arsenal isn’t in crisis. The thing about not being in crisis, though, is that when you’re really not in crisis, no one has to point it out. The zone in soccer between everything going well and everything collapsing into rococo nightmare is a strange and ambiguous state. The gap of daylight separating you from utter despair becomes a major topic of conversation. Your problems are endlessly discussed, but only in terms of your lingering hope of solving them.
